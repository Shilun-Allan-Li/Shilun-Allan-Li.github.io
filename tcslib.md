[TCSlib](https://shilun-allan-li.github.io/tcslib/) is a Lean 4 library for formalizing results in Theoretical Computer Science, built on top of [Mathlib](https://leanprover-community.github.io/mathlib4_docs/). Every theorem is fully machine-verified.

The project is part of UC Berkeley's [URAP](https://urap.berkeley.edu/) program, co-led by Prof. [Venkatesan Guruswami](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~venkatg/) and myself. The library is developed by me alongside a group of undergraduate researchers.

Areas currently covered:

- **Boolean Function Analysis**
  - Fourier analysis over the Boolean hypercube
  - Hypercontractivity
  - Arrow's theorem
- **Error-Correcting Codes**
  - Singleton, Hamming, Gilbert–Varshamov, and Johnson bounds
  - Linear codes
  - List decoding
  - Quantum codes
